Omega v1.0
(Confidential Document)
Omega is a proprietary protocol that is used to strengthen prison security and maintain them as
a Smart Penitentiary Facility.
To avoid problems such as possible prison breaks and rival gang member fights, we have fixed
different sensors including positioning devices, video cameras and perimeter monitors. If
something is detected by the sensor then the video cameras will turn to a given direction, and the
main gate will be closed.
All these devices work with Omega protocol, which is efficient and can be used flexibly across
devices. Each prisoner is closely monitored with video cameras, apart from a 10 minute
maintenance period during the day time. As this is critical period, we request the guards to move
out of the base and activate the laser systems (E1,E2 & E3). Any failure in laser systems will
trigger the alarms (A1, A2, A3 & A4) thereby alerting the guards, who will move to protect all
prison entrances.

Omega is a RF protocol. This protocol can be used in devices that operate at 1.8-5.5V, with a
frequency band in the range of 433.92 MHz. We have used an in-house Omega prototype to
convert RF signals to message format and vice versa.
