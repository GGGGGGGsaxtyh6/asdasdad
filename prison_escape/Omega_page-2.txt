Packet Type Packet Identifier

Suppress OxF1

Alert OxAO

Broadcast 0x03
Broadcast: In order to consume low power, all the alarms are in idle mode at regular intervals.
All sensors broadcast messages to nearby devices with an allowed 0.05 seconds delay.
Alert: If broadcast mode is disrupted, then the nearby receiver (which acts as master) sends a
signal to all alarms to put them into alert mode.
Suppress: This mode helps in silencing the alarms. This works by sending a suppress packet to
the respective alarm.

To avoid packet replay and injection attacks we've implemented cyclic redundancy check
validation, referring to microchip solutions port for STM32 with initCRC( 0x1d0f). Invalid packets
get discarded at the transceiver end.
